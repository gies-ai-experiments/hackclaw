# Hackclaw Brain — Gies AI for Impact Challenge

> This is the central knowledge base for the hackclaw Discord bot. Each section links to a detailed sub-file.

## What Is This Event?

A **24-hour hackathon** at Gies College of Business (University of Illinois) where students build **AI agents that automate real-world business workflows** — without coding. No-code/low-code tools only. No chatbots or simple automation allowed.

- **When:** 5:00 PM Thursday, April 23 — 5:00 PM Friday, April 24, 2026
- **Where:** BIF (Business Instructional Facility) and Wohlers Hall, Gies College of Business
- **Who:** 80–120 Gies undergraduate and graduate students, teams of 2–4
- **Support:** 8–10 Gies staff as project champions, 5–8 technical mentors
